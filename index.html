About Us

You must not lose faith in humanity. Humanity is an ocean; if a few drops of the ocean are dirty, the ocean does not become dirty.

VISION

Our vision is to see india one day having no person illiterate and no poverty .We are trying to make good citizens of India.Technological resources for contributing to the economic and social development of the nation at large and region in particular.

MISSION

Our mission is to erradicate illiteracy from India We are moving toward our mission by implementing this in IIIT Jabalpur by giving quality and value education to student of nearby villages. Our ultimate goal to extend our helping hand to each and every needy in India and make new India in the comming days.

The whole purpose of education is to turn mirrors into windows

PLANTATION

Trees are important, necessary and valuable to our very existence. It's not too hard to believe that without trees we humans would not exist on this beautiful planet. Unfortunately, our need for trees is more than ever. While science and technology has provided us with many comforts, the consequent urbanization and industrialization also has had some undesired side-effects; Global Warming being one of those. The Jagrati family has identified this problem and has associated itself with this case. Thereby, the Jagrati conducts a plantation drive each year.

MEDICAL CAMP

For this we guide their parents to take their children to doctors and take proper medicine. Health camps are also organized By us and general medicines are distributed free of cost to everyone.
